Slides (wraps) image content by specified (x,y) displacement. 

Creates a new image where pixel content is shifted by (x,y) pixels with wrap-\/around at image boundaries. This is performed with modular operations maintains the the coordinates when they are inside the boundaries and \char`\"{}wraps around\char`\"{} when they are outside the bound.
